Core Values

Prevention

CLINTERPOL believes that institutions should put the right proactive measures in place and to take appropriate foresighted and deliberate actions to reduce opportunities for crime, losses as well as negative security and safety incidences. CLINTERPOL believes that reactive firefighting responses are expensive, cumbersome, reputation damaging and actual recovery and restoration may be impossible to achieve.

Intelligence Led

CLINTERPOL believes that crucial and strategic decisions must be based not only on sufficient information but also that the information must be accurate and timely.

Justice

CLINTERPOL believes that fairness is all about the right of a client to make own decisions as to what should happen with its resources, that the acme of justice is restoration.

Integrity

CLINTERPOL believes that when clients are treated honestly, professionally, transparently, accountably, ethically, while giving them value for their investment, employing ethical business practices, being committed and diligent with their assignments, keeping to promised timelines and quality deliverables, then, CLINTERPOL has strategically positioned itself for its own growth and development.
